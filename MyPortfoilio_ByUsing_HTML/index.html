About Us

Hi, My name is Antriksh Saini,
I am a dedicated and passionate Java Full Stack Developer. I hold a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) from Govind Ballabh Pant Institute of Engineering and Technology, Pauri Garhwal (Uttarakhand). Although my academic background is in electronics, my strong interest in software development led me to specialize in full stack web development, particularly using Java technologies. I have built hands-on experience in both frontend and backend development through various projects and training.
I am always eager to learn new technologies, improve my skills, and work on challenging projects that help me grow both technically and professionally. I am looking forward to contributing to a dynamic development team where I can apply my knowledge, collaborate effectively, and grow as a full stack developer.
I enjoy creating dynamic, responsive, and efficient web applications that not only meet user requirements but also deliver a seamless user experience. My ECE background helps me understand systems and logic deeply, which I apply in my coding and application design.
